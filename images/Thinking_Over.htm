Thoughts On Just About Everything and Nothing At All

Over-Under is about my way of thinking. It's about my brain and my views on my life and the elements that surround it. I tend to overthink topics that shouldn't require much thinking (underthinking).

Are they petty? "Almost always" yes Are they necessary?" "Almost always" no Are they whole-heartedly mine? "Almost always" not really

See I have been influenced by a variety of different foces in my life, still I have enough content in this brain to ramble on about nothing for quite some time.
